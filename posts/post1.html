AI Natives and Generational Shifts

Published on: October 26, 2023 (or today's date)

"He seemed to be living in a different technological universe than I was. If the previous generation were digital natives, HudZah was an AI native."

Even though I'm fifty years old, I look around at most people I know, including young people, and I'm more of an AI native than they are. And that's what scares me. I should be the one trying to stay up with the young people. Instead, I know how to live in the new world that's emerging around us better than they do. That's not how generations are supposed to work.
